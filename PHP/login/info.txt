Agriculture is the most important sector of Indian economy. Indian agriculture sector accounts for 18 per cent of India's gross domestic product (GDP) and provides employment to 50% of the countrys workforce. India is the worlds largest producer of pulses, rice, wheat, spices and spice products. However, due to large biodiversity and environment conditions, a lot of crops suffer from diseases. Such diseases appear to be infectious amongst crops and spoil the harvest, thus creating losses for farmers. If proper care is not taken in this area then it causes serious effects on plants and due to which respective product quality, quantity or productivity is affected
